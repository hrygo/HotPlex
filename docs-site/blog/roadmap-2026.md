# Strategic Roadmap 2026

## Building the Standard for Stateful Agents

As we enter 2026, the AI agent landscape is shifting from "Proof of Concept" to "Production Reality." Our roadmap for the year is focused on three strategic pillars: **Trust, Scale, and Ubiquity.**

---

### Q1-Q2: The Trust Horizon

- **Secure Execution Sandboxes**: Launching the `v2` sandbox engine with isolated namespaces and fine-grained resource quotas.
- **Audit-as-a-Service**: Integrated session recording and deterministic replay for compliance and debugging.
- **Verified Hooks**: Signing and verification protocols for custom hook nodes.

---

### Q3: The Scale Horizon

- **Distributed State Store**: Move beyond singleton instances with our native clustering support for PostgreSQL and Redis.
- **High-Availability Engine**: Automated failover for the `hotplexd` daemon, ensuring zero downtime for your critical agents.
- **Multi-Model Orchestration**: Dynamic model routing and fallbacks based on performance, cost, and availability.

---

### Q4: The Ubiquity Horizon

- **Unified ChatApp Protocol**: A standardized JSON spec for building ChatApps, allowing any communication platform to speak "HotPlex" natively.
- **Visual Flow Orchestrator**: A high-design admin portal for orchestrating complex agent lifecycles via a drag-and-drop interface.
- **Mobile SDKs**: Bring the power of HotPlex to iOS and Android applications.

---
